Here is some important information about the dataset and its attributes. This information will help you to get a better understanding of the dataset.
# File extension - CSV(Comma Separated Values)
# Dimention of the dataset - 6687 rows * 29 Columns

#Attributes
1) Customer ID - The unique ID that identifies a customer
2) Churn Label - Contains “Yes” or “No” to indicate if a customer churned
3) Churn Reason - The particular reason why the customer ended the contract
4) Churn Category - Groups multiple churn reasons together for analysis purposes
5) Gender - The gender of the customer, indicated by “Male”, “Female”  or “Prefer not to say"
6) Under 30 - Indicates if the customer is under 30 with “Yes” or “No"
7) Senior - Indicates if the customer is 65 or above with “Yes” or “No”
8) Age - The age of the customer
9) Contract Type - Contains “Month to Month”, “One Year” or “Two Year”
10) Payment Method - The preferred payment method of the customer indicated with “Credit Card”, “Direct Debit” or “Paper Check"
11) State - The code of the state where the customer lives
12) Phone Number - Group Phone number of the customer
13) Group - Indicates if the customer is part of a group contract.  A group contract offers advantages and is generally cheaper. Contains “Yes” or “No”
14) Number of customers in a group - Number of customers part of the group
15) Account Length (in months) - The number of months the customer has been with Databel
16) Local Calls - Amount of local (within the US) calls from the customer
17) Intl Calls - Amount of international (outside the US) calls from the customer
18) Intl Mins - The number of minutes spent calling internationally. Intl Active: Indicates if the customer called internationally with a “Yes” or “No”
19) Intl Plan - Indicates if the customer has a premium plan to call internationally for free with “Yes” or “No. This premium is reflected in the amount of the monthly charge.
20) Extra International Charges - Contains the extra charges for international calls for customers who are not on an international plan.
21) Customer Service Calls - The number of calls made to customer service
22) Avg Monthly GB Download - Contains the average monthly download volume in gigabytes.
23) Unlimited Data Plan - Indicates if the customer has free unlimited download capacity with “Yes” or “No”. This premium is reflected in the amount of the monthly charge.
24) Extra Data Charges - Contains the extra charges for data downloads for customers who are not on an unlimited plan.
25) Monthly Charges - Average of all Monthly Charges to the customer
26) Total Charges - Sum of all monthly charges
